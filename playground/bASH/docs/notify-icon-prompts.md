# Notification Fallback Icon Prompts — Caricature Watercolor

One prompt per fallback icon. Drop into any text-to-image model. Shared style baseline: caricature illustration (exaggerated proportions, playful shapes) finished in watercolor — loose wet-on-wet washes, visible paper grain, ink contour accents, soft pigment bleeds, white paper used as highlight. Portrait aspect (~3:4) matching biome tiles (~444×590).

**No creatures, animals, humans, or fauna of any kind.** Plants, weather, terrain, light, statues, structures, and inanimate objects only.

---

## Encounter
**ID:** `encounter` — **Used by:** `notify_pokemon` when `sprite_path` missing
**Prompt:** Vertical close-up of a single chunky tuft of tall meadow grass shaking violently mid-rustle, blades bent in opposing directions as if something just dove in. Oversized cartoon exclamation mark "!" hovering above the tuft, casting a soft golden glow downward. Stray leaves and pollen motes flicking outward in arcs suggesting motion lines. No creature visible. Caricature: exaggerated swooshing grass blades, comically bulbous exclamation mark with chubby dot. Watercolor finish: fresh sap-greens and lime washes wet-on-wet, butter-yellow halo around the "!", ink contour on every blade tip, paper grain visible, splatter for kicked-up debris.

## Evolution
**ID:** `evolution` — **Used by:** `notify_evolution` when `sprite_path` missing
**Prompt:** Vertical shot of a single oversized luminous chrysalis cocoon split down the middle, brilliant white-blue light bursting outward through the crack. Radiating sparkle rays cutting across the frame, swirling ribbons of energy curling upward, weightless petals of light drifting. Empty interior — no creature emerging, only pure radiance. Caricature: exaggerated dramatic light shafts, oversized comic sparkles, chunky teardrop cocoon shape. Watercolor finish: cerulean and electric-violet wet-on-wet bleeds at edges, pure white paper held for the brightest core, lavender aura halo, gold-leaf shimmer flecks, ink contour on cocoon ridges.

## Level Up
**ID:** `level-up` — **Used by:** `notify_level`
**Prompt:** Vertical column of stacked floating runic stone tablets ascending skyward, each tablet glowing brighter than the one below it, a triumphant upward-pointing arrow etched on the topmost slab. A vertical god-ray sunbeam piercing down through the stack, sparkle motes spiralling upward along the beam, small pebbles and dust trailing behind the rising stones. No creatures present. Caricature: exaggerated tilted stacked tablets, oversized chunky arrow glyph, dramatic light shaft. Watercolor finish: warm honey-ochre stone washes, lemon-yellow inner glow, deep indigo background shadow, gold ink contour on glyphs, splatter sparkle accents, paper-white core highlights on the beam.

## Item
**ID:** `item` — **Used by:** `notify_item` when `sprite_path` missing
**Prompt:** Vertical shot of a small wooden treasure chest tipped slightly open on a mossy stone, spilling out an assortment of plump round berries, polished gemstones, and a single rolled parchment scroll. A warm golden sparkle rising from inside the chest, dust motes catching the light, tufts of grass and a curling fern frond framing the base. No creatures, no hands, only the inanimate loot. Caricature: exaggerated chubby chest with oversized iron hinges, comically fat round berries, dramatic curling scroll edges. Watercolor finish: honey-brown wood washes, ruby and emerald gem glazes, sap-green moss bleeds, butter-yellow inner glow, pure white paper highlights on metalwork, fine ink contour on planks and hinges, splatter for sparkle motes.
