# Biome Image Prompts — Caricature Watercolor

One prompt per biome. Drop into any text-to-image model. Shared style baseline: caricature illustration (exaggerated proportions, playful shapes) finished in watercolor — loose wet-on-wet washes, visible paper grain, ink contour accents, soft pigment bleeds, white paper used as highlight.

**No creatures, animals, humans, or fauna of any kind.** Plants, weather, terrain, light, statues, structures, and inanimate objects only.

---

## Forest
**ID:** `forest` — **Types:** grass, bug
**Prompt:** Wide shot of a lush deciduous forest clearing. Towering moss-skinned oaks with caricatured exaggerated heights, oversized fern fronds curling at the foreground, dappled sunbeams cutting through the canopy, fallen leaves layering the floor. Watercolor finish: layered wet-on-wet greens and warm ambers, wet-edge bleeds at canopy, fine ink linework for branches, paper grain visible throughout.
